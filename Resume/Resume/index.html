About Me

I'm a software developer specializing in enterprise level application architecture and development using the latest ASP.Net and cloud technologies. I obtained my Bachelors of Science in Mathematics with minor in Computer Science from the University of North Carolina at Greensboro in 2014, thouh I have development experience as early as 1998.
